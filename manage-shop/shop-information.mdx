# Shop information

Keep your shop's profile up to date so customers can find and learn about your coffee shop.

## Basic details

From the **Information** section in the Manage panel, you can edit:

- **Shop name** — Your coffee shop's display name
- **Description** — Tell customers about your shop. Supports rich text formatting on the web.

## Logo and cover photo

Upload a logo and cover photo to make your shop stand out on the directory and map.

- **Logo** — Displayed on your shop's page, loyalty cards, and map markers. Use a square image for best results.
- **Cover photo** — The banner image at the top of your shop's page. Use a 16:9 landscape image.

You can upload, replace, or remove both images at any time.

## Social media links

Add your social media links so customers can find you elsewhere:

- **Instagram** — Your Instagram handle
- **Facebook** — Your Facebook page URL
- **Twitter/X** — Your Twitter handle
- **TikTok** — Your TikTok handle
- **WhatsApp** — Your WhatsApp phone number (also used for online store orders)

## Tags

Tags help customers discover your shop through search and filtering. Select the tags that best describe your shop from the available options.

Halal certification tags are managed by the petakopi.my team — [contact us](/getting-started/contact) if you need to update your halal status.
