Set your shop's location so customers can find you on the map and get directions.

## Setting your location

From the **Location** section in the Manage panel:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for your shop">
    Use the Google Places search to find your shop by name or address. Selecting a result automatically sets the coordinates and links your Google Business profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fine-tune the pin">
    On the mobile app, drag the map pin to adjust your exact location. On the web, you can enter coordinates manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Tap **Save** to update your location. The state and district are automatically determined from your coordinates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Google Place linking

When you search and select your shop from Google Places, your shop becomes linked to your Google Business profile. This enables:

- **Sync opening hours** — Pull your operating hours from Google
- **Sync ratings** — Show your Google rating and review count on petakopi.my

If your shop doesn't appear in Google Places search, you can enter coordinates manually — but sync features won't be available until a Google Place is linked.
