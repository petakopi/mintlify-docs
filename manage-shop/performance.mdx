# Performance

The **Performance** page is the "how am I doing?" half of the manage panel. It pulls receipts from your Loyverse POS, lets you log cash expenses, and shows the bottom line.

<Info>This is the backward-looking half. The forward-looking half — setting prices and targets — lives in [Costs & Pricing](/manage-shop/costs-and-pricing).</Info>

## A single page, three tabs

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="All (default)">
    Period KPIs (gross sales, refunds, net revenue, expenses, true net, margin %) plus a chronological day-by-day breakdown showing receipts and expenses interleaved.

    **Use when:** doing a daily check — "what happened today / this week?"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sales">
    Filtered to receipts only: gross sales, refunds, net revenue, sale count. Day rows show receipt totals only.

    **Use when:** drilling into revenue patterns.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Expenses">
    Filtered to expenses: total, count, category breakdown bars. Flat list of expense entries with inline edit / delete and a one-tap **Add expense** button.

    **Use when:** logging cash expenses or reviewing where money went.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Period navigation

A month picker at the top of the page with presets:

- This week
- Last week
- This month
- Last month
- Custom range

The URL stays bookmarkable: `?tab=sales&month=2026-04`. Bookmark a specific month if you want to come back to it.

## Day drill-down

Click any date row to drill into a single day at `/performance/days/2026-04-15`. The day page has two tabs:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Receipts" icon="receipt">
    Read-only, sourced from your connected Loyverse POS. Shows line items and payment methods per receipt.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Expenses" icon="money-bill">
    Add, edit, or delete cash expenses. Photo upload supported (camera capture on mobile).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

For **past days** you also see a **"Pricing assumptions for this month"** collapsible at the top — it shows the Calculator inputs that were live for that month. Useful when you want to know "wait, why did the burn chart look that way back then?"

## What still gets emailed

The verdict mailers haven't changed. Cadence:

| When | Email | What's in it |
|---|---|---|
| Tue–Sun 8am MYT | Daily recap | Yesterday's facts only — no judgment |
| Mon 8am MYT | Weekly verdict | Last week's net, comparison to your trailing 4-week average, biggest expense mover |
| 1st of month 8am MYT | Monthly verdict | The prior month's net + margin (after ≥ 60 days of history) |

All email CTAs link to **Performance**.

## Adding a cash expense

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Expenses tab">
    Either from the Performance period view or from a specific day's drill-down.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add expense">
    Fill in: date, category, amount in MYR, vendor name, optional notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach a receipt photo (optional)">
    Tap the photo button. On mobile, opens the camera directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Appears immediately in the day's totals and in the period KPIs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quick reference

| I want to... | Go to |
|---|---|
| Check today's takings | Performance → All tab |
| See last week's net | Performance → Last week preset |
| Add a cash expense (with photo) | Performance → Expenses tab → Add expense |
| Drill into one day | Performance → click the date |
| Check what assumptions were live last month | Performance day-detail → "Pricing assumptions" panel |
| See per-recipe profit this month | [Recipes](/manage-shop/costs-and-pricing#loyverse-items) (after mapping Loyverse items) |
