The Manage panel is where you control your coffee shop's presence on petakopi.my. Access it by visiting your shop's page and tapping **"Manage"**.

## What you can manage

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Shop Information" icon="pen-to-square" href="/manage-shop/shop-information">
    Update your shop name, description, logo, cover photo, social media links, and tags.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Location" icon="location-dot" href="/manage-shop/location">
    Set your shop's location on the map and link to your Google Business profile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sync from Google" icon="rotate" href="/manage-shop/sync-from-google">
    Pull opening hours and ratings from your Google Business listing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Online Store" icon="store" href="/manage-shop/online-store">
    Set up products, manage orders, and configure your online store.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Costs & Pricing" icon="calculator" href="/manage-shop/costs-and-pricing">
    Set up items and recipes, calculate per-month prices, and link Loyverse items.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance" icon="chart-line" href="/manage-shop/performance">
    Track sales and expenses, review how your shop is doing month by month.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customer Feedback" icon="message" href="/manage-shop/customer-feedback">
    View and track feedback submitted by customers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Loyalty Card" icon="credit-card" href="/loyalty-card/overview">
    Run digital stamp programs for your customers.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Claim your shop">
    If you haven't already, [claim your coffee shop](/getting-started/claim-ownership) on petakopi.my.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Manage panel">
    Visit your shop's page on petakopi.my and tap **"Manage"**. You can also access it from the mobile app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start editing">
    Use the sidebar to navigate between sections. Changes are saved when you submit each form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>You can manage your shop from both the website and the mobile app. See [App vs Web](/manage-shop/app-vs-web) for a full feature comparison.</Info>
