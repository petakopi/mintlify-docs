# Costs & Pricing

The **Costs & Pricing** sidebar group is where you set up your menu, calculate what each cup costs you, and decide what to charge.

<Info>This is the "what should I sell, and at what price?" half of the manage panel. The other half — checking how you actually did — lives in [Performance](/manage-shop/performance).</Info>

## Mental model

```
Costs & Pricing
├── Items            ← raw materials (beans, milk, cups)
├── Recipes          ← sellable drinks built from items
├── Calculator       ← full P&L: volume × margin × costs → suggested prices
└── Loyverse Items   ← appears only when Loyverse is connected
```

You move down the list as you set up: ingredients → recipes → pricing math.

## Items — your raw materials

Every ingredient and consumable, with its pack price and yield %.

**Example:** Whole milk — pack size 1000ml, RM 8 per pack, yield 100% → app computes `unit cost = 0.8¢/ml`.

<Info>**Yield matters when waste is real.** Coffee beans usually waste ~10% to grind and spillage. Set yield to 90% and the app applies the waste to your COGS automatically.</Info>

## Recipes — your sellable drinks

Build a recipe by adding lines: each line is `{Item, quantity}`. The app sums to give you per-cup COGS.

Two prices appear:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Quick price" icon="bolt">
    On the Recipes form. Set a target food-cost % (e.g. 25%), the app shoots back a suggested sell price. Fast — but doesn't account for rent or wages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Calculator price" icon="calculator">
    On the Calculator page. Full P&L number — covers rent, wages, target margin, and your actual volume.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Use **Quick price** when you're sense-checking a single recipe. Use the **Calculator** when you're pricing the menu seriously.

## Calculator — full P&L per month

The Calculator page has a **month picker at the top**. Each month is its own snapshot — editing May doesn't change April.

### What you fill in per month

<Steps>
  <Step title="Volume">
    Either **Manual** (enter cups directly) or **Capacity-based** (estimated from seats × dwell time × occupancy × operating days × items per visit).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monthly costs">
    One row per line item: name, category (Labour / Overhead / Other), amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Target margin %">
    What you want left after all costs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What you get back

Per recipe: COGS, fixed cost per unit, suggested price.

Plus on the same page:
- **Actual this month** panel — your real numbers from Loyverse + manual expenses.
- **Burn chart** — actual revenue vs target, day by day.

### Per-month workflow

| Action | What happens |
|---|---|
| Visit a new month for the first time | Form pre-fills from your most recent prior month. Cue at top: "Cloned from April 2026 — adjust as needed." |
| Edit and save | Updates only that month. |
| Visit an old month | Shows what you had set then (or blank if you never set it up). |
| Edit a past month | One-time confirmation: "You're editing a past month. Saved changes will affect historical reports." |
| Plan ahead (future month) | Soft-warning banner shown. |
| **Apply forward to next N months** | Power-user shortcut to copy current month's settings forward. Max 11 months. |

## Loyverse Items — link variants to recipes

<Info>Visible only when your shop is connected to **Loyverse POS**.</Info>

Without linking: [Performance](/manage-shop/performance) shows total revenue. With linking: the **Recipes index** shows per-recipe sales count, revenue, and gross margin.

### How to map (one-time per variant)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Loyverse Items">
    Sidebar → **Loyverse Items**. Each row is a Loyverse variant (e.g. "Latte (Hot)", "Latte (Iced)") grouped by parent item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a Recipe">
    From the dropdown next to each variant. **Multiple variants can map to the same Recipe** — Hot + Iced Latte → one "Latte" recipe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync now (if needed)">
    The **Sync now** button at the top pulls the latest catalog from Loyverse. 5-minute cooldown between syncs to stay polite to the API.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What you get after mapping

- Recipes index gains three columns: **Sold this month**, **Revenue**, **Gross margin**.
- Backfill runs automatically — historical receipts that match the variant get attributed to the recipe within seconds.
- The Recipes form shows a read-only **"Linked Loyverse items"** block listing what's currently pointing at each recipe.

### Variants without a recipe

- Still count toward total Performance revenue.
- You just don't get the per-recipe breakdown for them.
- Sidebar shows an **"(N unmapped)"** badge so you don't forget.

## Quick reference

| I want to... | Go to |
|---|---|
| Add an ingredient | Items |
| Build a new drink | Recipes |
| Get a quick "what should I charge?" | Recipes form → Quick price |
| Get a "what should I charge to cover everything?" | Calculator |
| Set this month's volume / costs / margin | Calculator |
| See what next month's targets should be | Calculator → next-month picker |
| Map a Loyverse variant to a recipe | Loyverse Items |
| See per-recipe profit this month | Recipes (after mapping) |
