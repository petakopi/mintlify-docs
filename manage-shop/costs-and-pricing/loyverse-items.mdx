<Info>Visible only when your shop is connected to **Loyverse POS**. Not connected yet? See [Loyverse integration](/manage-shop/loyverse) for the connect flow.</Info>

Without linking: [Pulse](/manage-shop/pulse/overview) shows total revenue. With linking: the [Recipes index](/manage-shop/costs-and-pricing/recipes) shows per-recipe sales count, revenue, and gross margin.

## How to map (one-time per variant)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Loyverse Items">
    Sidebar → **Loyverse Items**. Each row is a Loyverse variant (e.g. "Latte (Hot)", "Latte (Iced)") grouped by parent item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a Recipe">
    From the dropdown next to each variant. **Multiple variants can map to the same Recipe** — Hot + Iced Latte → one "Latte" recipe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync now (if needed)">
    The **Sync now** button at the top pulls the latest catalog from Loyverse. 5-minute cooldown between syncs to stay polite to the API.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you get after mapping

- [Recipes index](/manage-shop/costs-and-pricing/recipes) gains three columns: **Sold this month**, **Revenue**, **Gross margin**.
- Backfill runs automatically — historical receipts that match the variant get attributed to the recipe within seconds.
- The Recipes form shows a read-only **"Linked Loyverse items"** block listing what's currently pointing at each recipe.

## Variants without a recipe

- Still count toward your total [Pulse](/manage-shop/pulse/overview) revenue.
- You just don't get the per-recipe breakdown for them.
- Sidebar shows an **"(N unmapped)"** badge so you don't forget.

Mapping is incremental: cover your top sellers first, come back to rare items later. The sidebar badge is your nudge — it ticks down as you map, but having unmapped variants doesn't break anything.
