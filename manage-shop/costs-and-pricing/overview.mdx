The **Costs & Pricing** sidebar group is where you set up your menu, calculate what each cup costs you, and decide what to charge.

<Info>This is the "what should I sell, and at what price?" half of the manage panel. The other half — checking how you actually did — lives in [Pulse](/manage-shop/pulse/overview).</Info>

## Mental model

```
Costs & Pricing
├── Items            ← raw materials (beans, milk, cups)
├── Recipes          ← sellable drinks built from items
├── Loyverse Items   ← appears only when Loyverse is connected
└── Calculator       ← full P&L: volume × margin × costs → suggested prices
```

You move down the list as you set up: ingredients → recipes → pricing math.
