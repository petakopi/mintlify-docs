# Loyalty Card for Customers

This guide covers how to get loyalty cards, collect stamps, and redeem rewards at your favourite coffee shops.

## Getting a loyalty card

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the shop's page">
    Find the coffee shop on [petakopi.my](https://petakopi.my) or in the mobile app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Add to Wallet'">
    On the shop's page, look for the loyalty card section and tap **"Get Loyalty Card"**.

    <Note>Screenshot will be added later</Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    You'll need to sign in to your petakopi.my account. You can sign in with your Apple or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a program">
    If the shop has multiple loyalty programs (e.g. "Coffee Card" and "Pastry Card"), select the one you want.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Earning stamps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Show your QR code">
    When making a purchase, show your loyalty card QR code to the shop staff. You can show it from:
    - The petakopi.my website or app
    - Apple Wallet or Google Wallet
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff scans your code">
    The staff will scan your QR code using the petakopi.my mobile app and award your stamps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Redeeming rewards

When you've collected all the stamps on your card:

1. The card is automatically marked as complete
2. Show your QR code to the staff as usual
3. The staff will redeem your reward through the app
4. A **new card is automatically issued** so you can start collecting stamps again

## Viewing your cards

You can view all your loyalty cards in two places:

- **On the web** — Visit your profile page on petakopi.my
- **On the app** — Open the petakopi.my app and tap **"My Cards"** on the Home tab

## Apple Wallet & Google Wallet

After adding a loyalty card, you can save it to **Apple Wallet** (iPhone) or **Google Wallet** (Android). Your stamp count updates in real time — you'll receive a push notification each time a stamp is added.

This means you can show your loyalty card directly from your phone's wallet without opening the petakopi.my app or website.
