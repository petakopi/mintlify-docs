# Quick start

Set up a digital loyalty card program for your coffee shop in three steps.

<Info>You can manage loyalty programs from both the website and the mobile app. This guide uses the website so you can get started right away without downloading anything. See [App vs Web](/loyalty-card/app-vs-web) for a full feature comparison.</Info>

## 1. Create your program

From the **[Manage ↗](https://petakopi.my/manage/)**, go to the Loyalty Card section and tap **"New Loyalty Program"**.

<Frame caption="Navigate to the Manage panel">
  <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-manage-panel.png" alt="Navigate to the Manage panel from your shop's page" />
</Frame>

<Frame caption="Go to Loyalty Programs and tap New Program">
  <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-loyalty-card-section.png" alt="Loyalty Programs section in the Manage panel with New Program button" />
</Frame>

Fill in the basics:
- **Program name** — e.g. "Buy 10 Get 1 Free"
- **Stamps required** — How many stamps to complete the card (1–10)
- **Expiration days** — How long cards stay valid (default: 365 days)
- **Terms & conditions** — What the reward is and any rules

Make sure the **Active** toggle is enabled — this allows customers to enroll in your program.

<Tip>You can skip the **Card Appearance** section for now — the default colors work great out of the box. See the [full owner guide](/loyalty-card/for-owners) if you want to customize your card colors later.</Tip>

Scroll down and tap **"Create Program"** to launch your loyalty program.

<Tip>You can run multiple programs at the same time — for example, a coffee card and a pastry card.</Tip>

## 2. Customers add the card

Once your program is active, a **"Get Loyalty Card"** button appears on your shop's page on petakopi.my.

<Frame caption="Get Loyalty Card button on your shop's page">
  <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-get-loyalty-card.png" alt="Get Loyalty Card button shown on a coffee shop page" />
</Frame>

Customers tap it, sign in, and the card is added to their account. They can also save it to **Apple Wallet** or **Google Wallet** for quick access.

<Tip>**Print a QR code for your counter!** Download one of our QR templates ([Design 1 ↗](https://assets.petakopi.my/media/QR_-_Design_1.png) or [Design 2 ↗](https://assets.petakopi.my/media/QR_-_Design_2.png)), generate a QR code linking to your shop's petakopi.my page using [QRCode Monkey ↗](https://www.qrcode-monkey.com/), and place it on the template. Print it and display at your counter so customers can scan it to get their loyalty card quickly.</Tip>

## 3. Scan and stamp

<Info>This guide uses the website scanner, but you can also scan using the [mobile app](/getting-started/download-app).</Info>

Open the menu on your shop's page and tap **"Scan QR"**, then tap **"Start Scanning"** to activate your camera.

<Frame caption="Open the menu, tap Scan QR, then Start Scanning">
  <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-scan-qr.png" alt="Open the menu, tap Scan QR, then tap Start Scanning" />
</Frame>

Point your camera at the customer's QR code. You can also tap **"Upload QR Image"** if you have a screenshot of the QR code.

<Frame caption="Tap Start Scanning to activate the camera">
  <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-scanner-ui.png" alt="Scanner UI showing Start Scanning button and active camera view with Upload QR Image option" />
</Frame>

Once scanned, the customer's loyalty card will appear. Select the number of stamps to award and tap **"Add Stamps"**. You'll see a confirmation once the stamps have been added.

<Frame caption="Select stamps to award and confirm">
  <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-add-stamps.png" alt="Loyalty card details with stamp selector and Stamps Added confirmation" />
</Frame>

When all stamps are collected, the card will show a **"Redeem Reward"** button. Tap it to redeem the customer's reward.

<Frame caption="Redeem the reward when the card is full">
  <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-redeem.png" alt="Card ready to redeem and reward redeemed confirmation" />
</Frame>

After redeeming, the customer needs to visit your coffee shop's page on petakopi.my and tap **"Get Loyalty Card"** again to get a new card and start collecting stamps.
