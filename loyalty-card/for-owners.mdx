This guide covers everything you need to know about setting up and running loyalty programs for your coffee shop.

<Info>New here? Start with the [Quick Start guide](/loyalty-card/quick-start) to get your first program running in minutes.</Info>

## Setting up a loyalty program

You can create a loyalty program from the **[Manage ↗](https://petakopi.my/manage/)** on the web or from the mobile app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Manage panel">
    Visit your shop's page, open the menu, and tap **"Manage"**, then navigate to the **Loyalty Programs** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new program">
    Tap **"New Program"** and fill in the details:
    - **Program name** — e.g. "Buy 10 Get 1 Free"
    - **Stamps required** — How many stamps to complete the card (1–10)
    - **Expiration days** — How long cards stay valid (default: 365 days)
    - **Terms & conditions** — What the reward is and any rules
    - **Active** — Toggle on to allow customers to enroll
  </Step>
  <Step title="Skip or customize card appearance">
    The form includes a **Card Appearance** section where you can customize wallet card colors. The defaults work well — you can always come back to customize later. See [Customizing card appearance](#customizing-card-appearance) below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the program">
    Scroll down and tap **"Create Program"**. Your program is now live and customers can start adding loyalty cards from your shop's page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can run **multiple programs at the same time** — for example, a coffee card and a pastry card with different stamp counts and rewards.

## Editing a loyalty program

You can edit your loyalty program from the Manage panel. However, once customers have active cards, some fields are locked to protect their progress:

| Field | Editable? | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Program name | Yes | Updates Apple Wallet passes immediately; Google Wallet updates on next stamp or new card |
| Terms & conditions | Yes | Updates appear on wallet passes after the next stamp |
| Expiration days | Yes | Only affects newly issued cards, not existing ones |
| Active status | Yes | Only controls whether new customers can enroll |
| Stamps required | No | Changing this would unfairly affect customers' progress toward their reward |

To change **stamps required**, you'll need to wait until all active cards have been redeemed or expired, or create a new program.

## Customizing card appearance

You can customize how your loyalty card looks in Apple Wallet and Google Wallet. This is available when creating a new program or editing an existing one.

Customize the following colors with a live wallet preview:
- **Background color** — Main card background color
- **Text color** (Apple only) — Text color on the card
- **Stamp area color** (Apple only) — Color behind the stamp grid

Google Wallet text color is automatically calculated for contrast.

## Scanning stamps

You can scan customer loyalty cards from the **website** or the **mobile app**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the scanner">
    **On the web:** Open the menu on your shop's page and tap **"Scan QR"**, then tap **"Start Scanning"**.

    **On the app:** Open the petakopi.my app and tap the **Scan** card on the Home tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the customer's QR code">
    Point your camera at the customer's loyalty card QR code. This can be shown from:
    - The petakopi.my website or app
    - Apple Wallet or Google Wallet

    On the web, you can also tap **"Upload QR Image"** if you have a screenshot of the QR code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Award stamps">
    Select the number of stamps to award and tap **"Add Stamps"**. You'll see a confirmation once the stamps have been added.

    <Frame caption="Select stamps to award and confirm">
      <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-add-stamps.png" alt="Loyalty card details with stamp selector and Stamps Added confirmation" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redeem completed cards">
    When all stamps are collected, the card will show a **"Redeem Reward"** button. Tap it to redeem the customer's reward.

    <Frame caption="Redeem the reward when the card is full">
      <img src="/images/loyalty-card/quick-start-redeem.png" alt="Card ready to redeem and reward redeemed confirmation" />
    </Frame>

    After redeeming, the customer needs to visit your shop's page on petakopi.my and tap **"Get Loyalty Card"** again to get a new card and start collecting stamps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing cards

From the Manage panel, you can:

- **View enrolled customers** — See who has added your loyalty card
- **Activity log** — Track all stamp and redemption activity
- **View individual cards** — See the stamp count for each customer

## Messaging loyalty card holders

You can send messages to customers who hold your loyalty cards. This is useful for announcing promotions, new menu items, or special events.

Messages are sent as notifications to your loyalty card holders through the petakopi.my app and website.
