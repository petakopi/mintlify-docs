## What you can do on the app

### For coffee lovers

- **Explore shops** — Discover coffee shops near you on the map or by browsing the directory
- **Coffee beans** — Browse and discover coffee beans from Malaysian roasters
- **Bookmarks** — Save your favourite shops for quick access
- **My Cards** — View your loyalty cards and stamp progress

### For shop owners

- **QR scanner** — Scan customer loyalty cards to award stamps and redeem rewards (accessible from the Home tab)
- **Manage your shops** — Quick access to your shop management
