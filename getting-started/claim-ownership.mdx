# Claim Your Coffee Shop

Claiming your coffee shop on petakopi.my gives you full control over your shop's listing and access to features like loyalty programs, an online store, and messaging.

## What you get as an owner

- **Manage panel** — Update your shop's details, photos, operating hours, and location
- **Loyalty programs** — Create digital stamp cards for your customers
- **Online store** — Sell coffee beans and merchandise through your shop page
- **Messaging** — Send updates and promotions to your loyalty card holders

## How to claim your shop

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your shop">
    Search for your coffee shop on [petakopi.my](https://petakopi.my). If your shop isn't listed yet, you can add it from the [Add Shop](https://petakopi.my/coffee_shops/new) page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact us">
    [Reach out to us](/getting-started/contact) to request ownership of your shop. Let us know which shop is yours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verification">
    We'll verify that you're the rightful owner of the shop and assign ownership to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After claiming

Once approved, you'll see a **"Manage"** button on your shop's page. This takes you to the Manage panel where you can:

- Edit your shop's details and photos
- Set up loyalty programs
- View and manage loyalty card holders
- Set up an online store
- Send messages to customers
