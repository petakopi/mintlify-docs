# Trip planner

The trip planner helps you discover coffee shops along your travel route. Plan your trip and find great stops for coffee between any two locations in Malaysia.

<Info>The trip planner is a **web-only** feature. Visit [petakopi.my/trip-planner](https://petakopi.my/trip-planner) to try it out.</Info>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    You need to be signed in to use the trip planner. You can sign in with your Apple or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your origin and destination">
    Enter your starting point and destination using the search box, or tap **"My location"** to use your current location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a search radius">
    Select how far from the route to look for coffee shops:
    - **1 km** (default) — Shops right along the route
    - **2 km** — A bit wider search
    - **5 km** — Includes shops further from the main road
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search">
    Tap **Search** to find coffee shops along your route. The results appear on the map and in a list panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reading the results

After searching, you'll see:

- **Route line** on the map showing your driving route
- **Coffee shop markers** with logos placed along the route
- **Results panel** listing all shops found, sorted by position along the route
- **Shop count** badge showing how many stops were found

Tap on any shop in the list or on the map to see its name, location, and a link to its petakopi.my page.

## Tips

- **Start with 1 km radius** — you'll get the shops closest to your route. Widen to 2 km or 5 km if you want more options.
- **Up to 50 shops** are shown per search. On popular routes, shops are spread evenly across the route so you see options for the whole journey.
- **Routes are cached** — searching the same origin and destination again is instant.

## Limits

- You can search up to **10 times per minute**. If you hit the limit, a countdown timer shows when you can search again.
- The trip planner requires a **sign-in** to use. Search inputs are disabled until you're logged in.
